Terms of Participation

  • Use of Data: Participants may freely use academic open datasets and the provided baseline mixture generation script. Any newly created or synthetic data must be disclosed by October 28, 2025 to ensure transparency and fairness.
  • Reproducibility: All submissions must include open-source training and evaluation code. A README.md file with reproduction steps and a repository link must be provided in the final submission.
  • Submission Limits: During the validation phase, participants may submit unlimited times. For the final phase, you may submit up to 3 final submissions.
